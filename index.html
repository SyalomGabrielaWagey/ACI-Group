ABOUT US
ANIMAL CARE INDONESIA GROUP A dedicated website for everyone who has a pet and wants to have one. Selling a variety of pet needs. ACI Group provides various types of highly nutritious food and drinks for your pets. There is also a health clinic service, for those of you who want to always ensure the comfort of your pet. In addition, the ACI group has a training ground for those of you who want to improve your pet's discipline and agility.For those of you who want to have a pet, don't worry. Because you can adopt it directly from our side. Because in addition to acting as a pet service center, ACI Group is also a shelter for abandoned animals and adoption facilities for those who want to raise pets directly.
